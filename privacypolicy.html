Tacoo Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026 · Contact: support@tacoo.club

This Privacy Policy explains how Tacoo protects information when users explore tattoo inspiration, use AI previews, and participate in the community.

Tacoo is a tattoo inspiration and AI tattoo preview app. The AI model is trained and managed by us, and uploaded images are not used to identify users.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect account information such as username, email address, profile photo, bio, age or date of birth, tattoo preferences, saved designs, and information you choose to provide. We may collect photos voluntarily uploaded for tattoo preview, community posts, profile images, or support requests. We may collect AI preview settings, selected tattoo designs, placement choices, generation history, feedback, comments, messages, reports, likes, follows, saves, shares, and purchase records.

2. Automatically Collected Information

When you use Tacoo, we may collect device model, operating system, app version, language, time zone, IP address, crash logs, diagnostics, network information, and device identifiers where permitted. We may also collect usage data such as pages viewed, tattoo categories browsed, AI preview activity, saved designs, community interactions, purchase interactions, and search activity. Security signals may be collected to detect abuse, fraud, spam, unauthorized access, and policy violations.

3. Device Permissions

Tacoo may request access to camera and photos so you can upload images for tattoo preview or community sharing. We may request notification permission for account, message, activity, generation, safety, or purchase notices. If future voice or video features are provided, microphone permission may be requested. You can change permissions in your device settings at any time, but some features may not work if permissions are disabled.

4. AI Model and Data Use

Tacoo uses internally managed AI models trained and optimized for tattoo preview and body-art visualization. The model is designed to apply tattoo designs, linework, shading, scale, and placement previews onto user-provided images. The AI system focuses on tattoo simulation and does not identify users, verify identity, or determine sensitive personal attributes.

5. No Collection for Identification

Tacoo does not use uploaded photos or AI inputs for face recognition, body recognition, biometric identification, authentication, surveillance, law enforcement profiling, or sensitive attribute inference. We do not create biometric templates, faceprints, bodyprints, identity databases, or long-term biometric profiles from uploaded content. Uploaded photos are processed only to provide the requested tattoo preview, display results, support moderation, troubleshoot errors, maintain security, and prevent misuse.

6. How We Use Information

We use information to provide tattoo inspiration, generate AI tattoo previews, operate community and messaging features, personalize recommendations, process consumable purchases, validate receipts, deliver digital items, prevent fraud, moderate content, enforce policies, maintain security, debug errors, improve reliability, respond to support requests, and comply with legal obligations.

7. Sharing of Information

We may share information with service providers that help operate Tacoo, including hosting, cloud storage, analytics, crash reporting, customer support, payment verification, content moderation, security, fraud prevention, and infrastructure providers. We may share purchase-related information with app stores and payment platforms. We may disclose information when required by law or when needed to protect users, Tacoo, or the public. We do not sell personal information in the ordinary sense.

8. Consumable Purchase Data

Tacoo may offer consumable digital items. We may receive purchase-related information such as product identifier, transaction identifier, purchase status, receipt validation data, country, delivery status, and refund or support records. Consumable purchases are not auto-renewable subscriptions and do not automatically renew. Purchase data is used to deliver items, prevent fraud, maintain records, handle support, and comply with legal or accounting requirements.

9. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy unless a longer period is required or permitted by law. If you delete your account, we will take steps to delete or anonymize personal information, subject to reasonable exceptions for legal obligations, dispute resolution, fraud prevention, moderation records, security, backups, and compliance.

10. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to processing, withdraw consent, or request portability of certain personal information. You may update some profile information in the app, revoke device permissions in device settings, or request account deletion by contacting support@tacoo.club. We may need to verify your identity before processing certain requests.

11. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, and disclosure. No mobile or internet service can guarantee absolute security, and you are responsible for protecting your account credentials and device security.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, AI model use, uploaded photo processing, account deletion, or privacy rights, contact us at support@tacoo.club.
