Tacoo Community

Community Guidelines & EULA

Last updated: June 9, 2026 · Contact: support@tacoo.club

These guidelines explain how users should behave in Tacoo’s tattoo inspiration, AI preview, and community features.

Tacoo is a tattoo inspiration and AI tattoo preview app. The AI model is trained and managed by us, and uploaded images are not used to identify users.

1. Community Purpose

Tacoo is intended to help users explore tattoo inspiration, preview ideas with AI, share body-art concepts, discuss designs, follow creators, and participate in a positive tattoo-focused community. These guidelines apply to all uploads, AI tattoo previews, community posts, comments, messages, profile pages, reports, and interactions on Tacoo.

2. Respectful Conduct

Users must treat others respectfully. Harassment, bullying, intimidation, threats, hateful conduct, stalking, malicious false reporting, or repeated hostile behavior are prohibited. Users may not attack or degrade people based on race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, sex, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, medical condition, age, immigration status, veteran status, or similar protected characteristics.

3. Photo Consent and Privacy

Do not upload another person’s image, tattoo photo, body photo, or private reference without permission. This includes friends, strangers, customers, models, public figures, minors, or people appearing in group photos. You may not share another person’s private information, including addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, private messages, IDs, payment details, studio records, or confidential artist/client information.

4. AI Tattoo Preview Rules

Users may not use AI preview features to create harmful, deceptive, harassing, sexual, exploitative, illegal, impersonating, or privacy-invasive content. Users may not attempt to bypass safety systems, manipulate AI behavior, generate misleading identity-related content, or use AI outputs to defame, deceive, or harass others.

5. Tattoo Safety Rules

Tacoo content is for digital visualization and inspiration. AI tattoo previews do not guarantee real-world tattoo safety, artistic outcome, healing result, or suitability. Users should consider allergies, skin sensitivity, infection risk, ink ingredients, aftercare, placement restrictions, local laws, and professional tattoo advice before making real-world tattoo decisions.

6. Prohibited Content

Prohibited content includes illegal activity, violent content, threats, hate speech, harassment, sexually explicit or exploitative content, non-consensual intimate content, scams, phishing, impersonation, misleading promotions, private information exposure, copyright-infringing tattoo designs, spam, bots, fake engagement, scraping, and platform manipulation.

7. Intellectual Property

Users should only upload tattoo photos, flash sheets, design references, logos, images, or creative materials that they own or are authorized to use. Unauthorized use of copyrighted tattoo designs, artist portfolios, studio works, celebrity images, brand logos, or third-party materials may result in removal or account enforcement.

8. Messaging and Artist Interaction

Messaging and community interactions must remain respectful and tattoo-focused. Users may not harass artists, pressure users, solicit illegal services, send spam, request sensitive personal data, or use chat features to abuse others. Advice received through Tacoo is general creative guidance and should not replace professional medical, legal, or licensed tattoo consultation.

9. Consumable Purchases and Fair Use

Consumable digital items must be used honestly and in accordance with platform rules. Users may not exploit purchase systems, manipulate item delivery, abuse refunds, or use purchased items to harass or manipulate others.

10. Reporting and Moderation

Users are encouraged to report harmful content, AI misuse, privacy violations, copyright infringement, impersonation, harassment, scams, unsafe tattoo claims, or other misuse. Tacoo may review reports using manual review, automated tools, safety signals, purchase records, moderation history, and community reports.

11. Enforcement

Tacoo may take enforcement action at its discretion, including warnings, content removal, reduced visibility, messaging restrictions, AI generation restrictions, purchase restrictions, temporary suspension, permanent account termination, or preservation of records for safety and legal purposes. Severity, repetition, harm level, legal obligations, and App Store compliance requirements may affect enforcement decisions.

12. Contact

Questions about these guidelines may be sent to support@tacoo.club.
